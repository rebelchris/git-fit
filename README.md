Git-Fit monitors your developer workflow and turns the "Progress Bar Boredom" into a high-intensity micro-workout.

## How it works

- Detection: Git-Fit observes when high-CPU processes (like xcodebuild, cursor, or docker) are active.
- The Interrupt: When you switch away from your IDE while an agent is working, Git-Fit triggers a "Commit to Gains" session.
- The Workout: 20-60-second exercises designed for people in hoodies.

## Why is there no code here?

Note: This repository currently serves as the distribution hub for the Git-Fit macOS binary.

## Privacy & Security:

- Local-First: We don't have a backend. Your PRs (Personal Records) stay on your machine.
- Zero Tracking: No analytics. No telemetry. Just you and the iron.
- Process Monitoring: We use local system APIs to detect active dev tools. We never read your code or keystrokes.
